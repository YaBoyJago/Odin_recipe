Classic Caesar Salad

Description

A timeless favorite, the Classic Caesar Salad is crisp, refreshing, and bursting with bold flavors. Made with crunchy Romaine lettuce, a creamy garlic dressing, croutons, and grated Parmesan, this salad is perfect as a starter or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of Romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the Romaine lettuce thoroughly.
  2. In a large salad bowl, toss the lettuce with Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  3. Top with croutons and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Sprinkle with freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
